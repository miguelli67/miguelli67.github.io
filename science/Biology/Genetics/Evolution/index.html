Introduction

Evolution is the process through which species change over time. These changes accumulate and can lead to the formation of new species.

Mechanisms of Evolution

  • Natural Selection: Organisms better adapted to their environment survive and reproduce.
  • Mutation: Changes in DNA that introduce variation.
  • Gene Flow: Movement of genes between populations.
  • Genetic Drift: Random changes in gene frequency in small populations.

Evidence of Evolution

  • Fossil Record
  • Comparative Anatomy
  • Molecular Biology
  • Embryological Similarities
